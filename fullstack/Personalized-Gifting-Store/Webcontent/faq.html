Frequently Asked Questions

How to place an order from the website?
  1. Start by clicking on your preferred item category. For example, if you're looking for bouquets, click on the 'Flowers' tab. Scroll through the options and click on your desired product.
  2. On the product page, either click "Add To Cart" to continue shopping for tokens or "Buy Now" to proceed to checkout. Select add-ons if desired.
  3. Review your selections by clicking the "Cart" tab in the top right corner of the web page. Next, click "Place Order."
  4. Log in with your email ID as prompted. On the checkout page, provide the delivery address, choose the delivery date/time, and add a special message. Click "Review."
  5. Verify your order and click "Proceed To Payment" to complete your purchase.
What are the different modes of payment available, and can payment be made through cash?

To make this process convenient for you, we accept payment through Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Digital Wallets, UPI and Net Banking. However, we do not offer the cash on delivery option for ordering.

I am not able to complete the payment. What should I do?

If you're encountering this issue, it may be related to a connectivity problem. To resolve this, ensure that you have a stable internet connection when placing your order.

If the issue continues to persist, please don't hesitate to reach out to our dedicated support team at wecare@floweraura.com or give us a call at 9345665456.

In which cities do you provide song dedication service “Floral Notes”?

Our Floral Notes service is provided only in Hyderabad.
